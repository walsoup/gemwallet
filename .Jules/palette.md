## 2026-04-16 - Add accessibilityLabel to react-native-paper icon components
**Learning:** Icon-only components like `IconButton`, `FAB`, and `TextInput.Icon` in `react-native-paper` require explicit `accessibilityLabel` props to be readable by screen readers.
**Action:** Always provide `accessibilityLabel` for icon-only buttons in `react-native-paper` applications for proper accessibility support.
