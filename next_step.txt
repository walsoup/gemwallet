Here's a full breakdown of what to add and enhance next for GemWallet:

---

## 🔥 High-Impact Features to Add Next

### 1. **Budgets & Spending Limits** *(Biggest UX gap)*
Your ledger tracks spending but has no guardrails. Add per-category monthly budgets with a visual progress ring on each category chip. When you hit 80%+, trigger a haptic warning and a color shift to `errorContainer`. This makes the AI analysis far more meaningful too — it can compare actuals vs. limits.

### 2. **Recurring Transactions**
No finance app feels complete without this. Add a `recurring` flag + `frequency` field (`daily | weekly | monthly`) to the `Transaction` type. A background task (via `expo-task-manager`) auto-logs them on schedule. The ledger can show a ⟳ icon on recurring items.

### 3. **Multi-Wallet / Accounts**
Right now there's a single balance. Adding wallet accounts (Cash, Bank, Card) with a `walletId` on each transaction unlocks "transfer between accounts" flows and per-account balance views. The Hero Card can become swipeable between wallets.

### 4. **Charts & Analytics Screen**
You have AI analysis but no visual data. A dedicated `/analytics` route with:
- Monthly bar chart (spending by month)
- Donut chart (breakdown by category)
- Income vs. Expense line chart

Use `react-native-gifted-charts` — it's lightweight, performant at 120Hz, and has no SVG dep (fits your pruning philosophy).

### 5. **Tags / Notes Enhancement**
Currently notes are freeform. Add a `tags` array to transactions so users can cross-cut across categories (e.g. `#work`, `#vacation`). Tags become filterable chips, and the AI can do tag-based pattern analysis.

---

## 🧠 AI Pipeline Enhancements

### 6. **Proactive AI Nudges**
Instead of on-demand analysis, trigger a subtle AI insight card on the dashboard after every 5th transaction. Short, punchy (1 sentence), inline — not a full modal. Something like: *"You've spent 3× more on food this week than last. 👀"*

### 7. **Voice-to-Transaction**
Add `expo-speech` or the device mic + Whisper (via Gemini's audio input) so users can say *"Spent 12 dollars on coffee"* and it parses directly into the NLP pipeline you already have. The infrastructure is there — it just needs an audio input layer.

### 8. **Smarter AI Context**
Right now you inject the full transaction history as a raw JSON blob. Improve this by pre-aggregating before sending: totals per category, weekly deltas, top 5 largest expenses. This reduces tokens, speeds up streaming, and makes the AI's analysis more focused.

---

## 🎨 Design & Motion Polish

### 9. **Transaction Detail Sheet**
Tapping a transaction currently does... what? Add a bottom sheet showing full detail — amount, category, date, note, and an **Edit** option. Right now editing seems absent entirely, which is a critical gap.

### 10. **Swipe-to-Delete on Ledger Items**
You have an undo snackbar but no swipe gesture on list items. Add `react-native-gesture-handler`'s swipe-left to reveal a delete action — this is more intuitive than a long-press or separate flow.

### 11. **Lottie Celebrations**
On milestone events (first transaction, first $100 saved, first income logged), trigger a quick Lottie confetti burst. `lottie-react-native` is small and Expo-compatible. Ties into your existing haptic success signature.

### 12. **Widget / Home Screen Glance** *(Android)*
With Expo's new widget support, a home screen widget showing current balance + today's spend would be a killer differentiator. Read-only from the Zustand persist store.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Debt & Infrastructure

### 13. **Edit Transactions**
There's no mention of editing existing transactions — only add/delete/undo. Add an `updateTransaction` action to the store and surface it via the detail sheet (item #9 above).

### 14. **iCloud / Google Drive Backup**
The CSV export is manual. Add an optional auto-backup that serializes the Zustand store to JSON and pushes to the user's cloud on a schedule. Privacy-preserving (user's own cloud, not your servers) — fits your offline-first mandate perfectly.

### 15. **Biometric Lock**
Add `expo-local-authentication` (Face ID / fingerprint) as an optional app lock in Settings. One toggle, massive trust signal for a financial app.

### 16. **Deep Link / Share Extension**
Allow sharing a receipt image or text from another app directly into GemWallet's NLP parser. Android share intents + iOS share extension via Expo's `expo-intent-launcher`.

---

The single most important thing to build **right now** is the **Edit Transaction flow** — it's a functional gap that will frustrate any real user. After that, the **Analytics screen** and **Budgets** are what turn a simple ledger into an actual personal finance tool.
